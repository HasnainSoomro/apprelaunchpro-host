Privacy Policy for AppRelaunch Pro

Last Updated: September 1, 2025

Overview

AppRelaunch Pro ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use our app monitoring and auto-restart service designed for Fire TV, Android TV, and other Android devices.

Information We Collect

Information We DO Collect

Device and App Information:

  • List of installed applications on your device (to populate the app selection dropdown)
  • Package names of apps you choose to monitor
  • App crash/stop detection data (processed locally only)
  • Device system information necessary for app functionality

Usage Data:

  • App monitoring settings (target app, timeout intervals, enable/disable status)
  • Service operation logs (stored locally for troubleshooting)

Information We DO NOT Collect

AppRelaunch Pro is designed with privacy in mind. We DO NOT:

  • Collect any personal information or user data
  • Access the content of other apps
  • Monitor your browsing history or personal activities
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or personal files
  • Send any data to external servers
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements or collect advertising data

How We Use Information

The information collected is used solely for:

  1. App Monitoring: Detecting when your selected app stops running or crashes
  2. Auto-Restart Functionality: Automatically relaunching your chosen app when needed
  3. Service Configuration: Saving your preferences for timeout intervals and target apps
  4. System Integration: Ensuring proper operation with Android system services

All data processing occurs locally on your device only. No information is transmitted to external servers or third parties.

Data Storage and Security

  • All settings and preferences are stored locally on your device using Android's secure SharedPreferences
  • No cloud storage or external databases are used
  • App monitoring data is processed in real-time and not permanently stored
  • Service logs are kept locally for troubleshooting and are automatically cleared over time

Permissions Explained

AppRelaunch Pro requests the following permissions for legitimate functionality:

Internet & Network Access:

  • Used for potential future updates and service communication
  • Currently no data is transmitted over the network

System Alert Window (Overlay):

  • Required on some Android versions for proper service operation
  • Ensures the app can function correctly in the background

Boot Completed:

  • Allows the service to restart automatically after device reboot
  • Ensures continuous monitoring of your selected app

Foreground Service:

  • Enables reliable background monitoring
  • Required for Android's background execution limits compliance

Query All Packages:

  • Necessary to display the list of installed apps for selection
  • Used only to populate the app selection dropdown

Third-Party Services

AppRelaunch Pro does not integrate with any third-party services, analytics platforms, or advertising networks. The app operates entirely independently without external dependencies.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or transfer any information to third parties. All functionality is contained within the app and your device.

Children's Privacy

AppRelaunch Pro does not collect any personal information from users of any age. The app is designed as a utility tool and does not require or request personal data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last Updated" date above. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Other Jurisdictions:
AppRelaunch Pro's privacy-first design ensures compliance with most regional privacy regulations.

  • Secure local storage for user preferences
  • Standard Android APIs for app management

Since AppRelaunch Pro does not collect personal data, traditional data subject rights (access, deletion, portability) are not applicable. However, you maintain full control over:

  • Which apps you choose to monitor
  • Timeout intervals and monitoring settings
  • Enabling or disabling the service entirely
  • Uninstalling the app at any time (which removes all local data)

Data Retention

  • Service logs: Automatically cleared periodically
  • No long-term data storage or retention policies needed due to local-only operation
